Various Tools

Every tool has its own different properties to help you get what you want done

MasterNote provides you with many different tool; the basic tools like the pen, eraser and highlighter, and tools to draw shapes and select items.
Each tool has many different properties!

Powerful File System

Mange and organize your files like if they are in a real library!

MasterNote has a very powerful file system, you can create, rename, duplicate or delete libraries and books. This helps you orgianize your files easily and in a practical way.

Export or Share Your Files

Backup your files or share them with your friends

MasterNote provides you with the ability to take backups of your data (libraries and books).
MasterNote also makes it easy for you to share your handwritten notes with your friends using different social media platforms.
